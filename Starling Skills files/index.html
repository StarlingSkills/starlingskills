About Starling Skills

We offer online training with masters-qualified teachers to develop English language communication skills. Better, clearer communication means a more efficient, professional and happier team. We provide a range of courses with focused outcomes to deliver real results in a six or twelve-hour training course. Don’t see what you’re looking for? We will create a specially designed course around your objectives and context.

We believe in a communicative, socially-constructed theory of language and learning. Students work together and learn from each other. Given the multicultural nature of the modern workplace, learning how to best share a common understanding is more important than ever.

Our courses are designed for small classes of three to twelve students to work with our expert teachers using video conferencing software. We also adapt to use the online tools you use in your workplace, facilitating the essential connection between language and digital literacy.

Courses

We don’t believe in learning long lists of words we will never use or grammar rules that do nothing but confuse. We teach vocabulary and grammar in the context you will use it. Our courses are short because we focus on specific aspects of work-based communication. This focus delivers clear outcomes and tangible results.

Take the course with your team and work together to improve the way you communicate and work. No matter the differences in level, we create a cooperative supportive environment to learn together.
