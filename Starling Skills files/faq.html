Questions About Classes

  • I've never learned a language online, how is it different and what should I expect?

    Learning online is undoubtedly different from learning face to face. We communicate differently depending on our mode of communication. That is to say that the way you communicate is different on the phone as it is sending emails or speaking to someone face to face. We aim to help you gain confidence in these different and unique forms of communication that have become normal. Most English classes don’t recognise this difference and therefore aren’t teaching you the English and communication skills you need.

  • Do you teach beginners or lower levels?

    Our classes are designed for people who already work or are about to start working in English. That means students need to be at least B1. However, send us a message and we can create a custom-made course for you to meet your needs.

  • Can native speakers join?

    Yes. If they are part of the team we encourage it. Everyone can improve their communication skills and often native speakers have no idea how or when they can cause others difficulty. Native speakers will also form an important part of your work culture and we think they have an important voice. Our classes encourage collaboration between all levels to promote better communication.

  • How do you judge my level?

    We ask all students to complete an online questionnaire before starting. This is based around the CEFR (Common European Framework Reference) can-do statements. If we are unsure, we may do a further diagnostic.

    If you want something more detailed, we can do a full diagnostic, see our services section for more information.

  • Can I do one-to-one classes?

    We are happy to design you a custom-made one-to-one course. Send us a message and tell us what skills you are interested in developing.

  • Will I have one or more teachers on my course?

    We do our best to keep the same teacher on the complete course. We believe it is the best way to build a relationship and for our teacher to understand your needs.

  • Can I choose my teacher?

    Of course! Most classes have a fantastic experience with our teachers and we do our best to keep you together on your next course.

  • Why is three the minimum class size?

    We believe in a cultural social approach to language learning. That means our courses are designed around communicating with other people. Furthermore, we communicate with multiple people differently from how we communicate one to one. We recommend a class size of 4-8 learners.

  • Why is twelve the maximum class size?

    We believe teacher student interaction is important. Having more that twelve students would mean we couldn’t provide a full and fair service to everyone.

  • What is the difference between the six and twelve-hour courses?

    Our six-hour course squeezes in the most important content into a fast-paced intensive course. The twelve-hour course has more content and more space to practice and get feedback on your progress.

  • I have done the six-hour class but want to do more. Can I do the twelve-hour class?

    Sure. We will upgrade you to the twelve-hour class and we only charge the difference.

  • How do you know my level before an IELTS class?

    For our standard six and twelve hour courses, we will send you an online questionnaire. If your level is below B1 but you still want to prepare for the exam, we can design you a custom-made course.

  • How long will it take me to do an IELTS class?

    Our IELTS classes follow our standard six and twelve hour model. Remember if you feel you need more time we can design you a custom-made course.

  • What happens if I don't get the band I want?

    We offer no guarantees on the score you’ll get. What we can guarantee is our honest and professional advice and fantastic classes to help you do as well as you possibly can.

  • Are the lessons recorded so I can watch them back again?

    We do not routinely record classes. If you wish to record the classes, we would require the written permission of all attendees and your organisation.

  • Will the teacher give me material, or do I need to buy a book?

    There is no need to buy any books or extra material. All class material is provided by the teacher and shared in the way that best suits you.

  • What time are your classes?

    We are flexible to your schedule. We can change the time and frequency of your classes to suit you.

  • How often are classes?

    Whatever you want. We can do once a week, every day, or even intensive one or two day courses. We recommend once or twice a week as this gives you time to assimilate new skills and ask for feedback as you try things out.

  • Can I do a six-hour course in one day?

    Sure. The courses are designed with this kind of flexibility in mind.

  • What happens if I have a class and I need to change the time?

    Let us know two working days in advance and we can reschedule the class.

  • What happens if the teacher is sick?

    We will do our best to find a substitute teacher or if you prefer, we can reschedule the class.

Questions About Services

  • Why do I need to do services like proofreading on a video call?

    We don’t just proofread and correct your writing; we want to make you a better writer and better proof-reader. By talking you through the process, we will tell you not just what but also why. Also, we are not experts in your field, by discussing the meaning we can be sure you are saying what you want to say.

  • How do you calculate the cost of proofreading?

    This depends on the length and the type of document you want proofed and how quickly you need it. Send us a message with these details and we’ll give you a quote.

  • Does the quality of my English matter? For example, if something is very poorly written does it still cost the same?

    The price remains the same but we will obviously not be able to focus on all the errors in the consultation. The better your draft, the better your learning experience will be.

  • Do you proofread for both American and British spelling and punctuation?

    Our standard is British English but we are happy to proof in American English.

  • How fast is the turnaround for proofreading? I need something done ASAP, can I pay for a faster service?

    We can provide a faster service but remember our focus is not on the document but on you. If you need something very quickly and don’t have time to develop your language, we might not be the service for you.

  • What happens if I don't understand the proof-reader’s comments?

    Don’t worry. Unlike other services, we give you the chance to talk directly to your proof-reader and make sure you understand exactly what you are saying.

  • How does the proof-reader mark my page - track changes?

    Proofreading is done using Microsoft Word and we use track changes and comments.

  • Do you just correct grammar and vocabulary or will you look at the content of the document?

    We look at all linguistic aspects of the document. That includes meaning, so if something isn’t clear we will highlight it and discuss it with you.

  • How many people can join the teambuilding?

    The maximum size for team building is 50. We can manage this by having multiple facilitators. We maintain a maximum ratio of 12:1 student to facilitator.

Questions About Technology

  • We don’t use Zoom in our company. Can we still attend class or use your services?

    Our preference is to use Zoom as we believe it offers better functionality. However, we can also use Skype, Teams and Google Meet. We are also happy to investigate using other options should you require.

  • What happens if there are internet issues during the lesson?

    If we are having a problem, we will happily extend the session so you will still meet your learning aim. If the issue lasts longer than 15 minutes, we will reschedule the class. Unfortunately we cannot be responsible for your connection but we try to be as flexible as possible and will extend the class whenever we can.

  • How fast does my internet connection need to be?

    We recommend a minimum of 3.8Mbps/3.0Mbps (up/down) for more information on system requirements for Zoom we recommend you visit this site here. We recommend having a work meeting with your cameras on. If it works, there will be no problem.

  • What kind of technology do I need?

    You will need a computer, microphone and speaker or headphones and a webcam. While you can connect using a tablet or smartphone, you will have reduced functionality.

  • Why do I need a camera when I don’t use it in work?

    That is a good question. There will be times when we will ask you to turn it off to replicate your work experience. However, facial expressions are an important part of communication and help build confidence and trust between participants. As you will be working with your teacher and colleagues as a group, we feel it is important to build a trustful and supportive environment. The more relaxed you are the better your English will be.

Questions About Pricing and Billing

  • How much is a class?

    Our pricing depends on the course you take and the options you choose. Send us a message and we’ll give you a quote.

  • What currency do you charge in?

    We are based in the UK so we charge in GB Pounds. However, we will give you a binding quote when you first enquire about the classes meaning it is easy for you to budget.

  • How do I pay? Are there online options?

    Our standard payment model is invoice and bank transfer. However, we also accept credit and debit card payments using PayPal.

  • Can I pay for multiple classes at once or can I pay one class at a time?

    Classes are booked in six or twelve-hour blocks and must be paid when the class is booked. If you are paying by bank transfer we will send you an invoice which must be paid before the start of the course.

  • Can I buy classes for someone else?

    Of course. Once you have made your purchase, we will ask you to send us the names and email addresses of the students. If you are unsure of their availability, we are happy to arrange directly with them. All classes must however be completed within six months of purchase.

  • Can I take a class with my friend? For example, two people sharing the cost of one IELTS preparation?

    Our class size is three to twelve students, the cost per class remains the same. If there are only two of you, we can make you a custom-made course.
